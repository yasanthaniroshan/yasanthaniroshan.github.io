**PosturePulse** is an AI-based physiotherapy assistant that watches a patient exercise and provides **real-time feedback** on their form, along with personalized exercise programs.

The project placed **2nd runner-up at Brainstorm 2024**, the healthcare-innovation competition organized by IEEE EMBS at the University of Moratuwa. A full walkthrough of the system is available in the demo video linked above.
